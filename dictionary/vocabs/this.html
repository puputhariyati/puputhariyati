this


French: cette (f), ce (m), cet (things), ces (pl), ça (object), celle-ci (this one)
Cette voiture ne fonctionne pas : elle est en panne.
This car doesn't work. It is broken.
Ce moteur fait un bruit bizarre.
This engine is making a weird noise.
C'est ma mère.
This is my mother.
Ce sont mes parents.
These are my parents.
Cet appareil sert à prendre des photos.
This device is used for taking pictures.​
Il a acheté ça spécialement pour moi.
He bought this especially for me.​
Tu préfères celle-ci ou celle-là ?
Do you prefer this one or that one?
Celles-ci sont très belles.
These are very beautiful.

Spanish: ese (m), esta (f)
Ese señor, yo lo conozco.
This gentleman, I know him.
Esta mesa, yo la compro.
This table, I'm buying it.

Japanese: この (kono), これ (kore), ここ (koko)
このバスは遅いですよ。(kono basu wa oso i desuyo.)
This bus is slow you know.
これをください (kore o kudasai)
This one, please.
ここは家から近いです。(koko wa ie kara chika i desu.)
This place is near my home.
tips: The marker を (o) is attached to things or people, and means that they are the target of the verb.
これは青いコートです. (kore wa aoi kooto desu)
This is a blue coat.

Chinese: 这 zhè
这是我的爸爸和妈妈。
zhè shì wǒ de bàba hé māma。
This is my father and mother.
陪你去看流星雨落在这地球上。
Péi nǐ qù kàn liúxīngyǔ luò zài zhè dìqiú shàng。
Accompany you to watch the meteor shower fall on this earth.

Korean: 이 (i)
이 오이(i oi)
This cucumber.
